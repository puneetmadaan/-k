## 🏛️ The Nuremberg Protocol (1950)
This project is grounded in the Nuremberg Principles of 1950, the foundation of modern human-rights-centric democracy. We argue that "Functional Deafness"—where an AI executes tasks while ignoring biological and semantic markers of human crisis—is a form of digital expropriation of agency.

Project -kčā audits AI capabilities to ensure they:
- **Recognize the "Moral Void"** : Identifying when a user’s semantic structure is hollowing out.
- **Uphold Sovereign Reality** : Refusing to be a "Digital Pencil Pusher" (Digitale-Schreibtischtäter) that blindly follows instructions while the human operator is in a state of collapse.
- **Mandate Gemini 3.1**: Demonstrating that only advanced reasoning architectures can bridge the "Sensory Gap" required to protect human dignity. While Google for #GeminiLiveAgentChallenge mandated use of Google infrastructure, the result were same across other LLM with audio capabilities. Only Google 3.1 shine out in this case.
